About Me

I was born and raised in Putrajaya, Malaysia. Loving my own country, my parents finally had the opportunity to work and live in Japan for 3 years, and so I followed. Journey in Japan was tough, I had to find new friends who wasn't the same race, speaks the same language and share the same culture with me. There were more to that, I also had to endure and experience Japan's worst earthquake and tsunami in 2011. Although, life was tough eventhough I was young, I beleive it was a turning point in my life. All these tragedy has taught me to be disciplined, mature and always rely on my own.


A short introduction of myself, I am currently a first year student of Queen Mary University of London studying BSc Computer Science.
